# Telecom Customer Churn Analysis

## Project Title

**Telecom Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard using Power BI**

---

## Short Purpose of the Project

The purpose of this project is to analyze customer churn patterns in a telecom company and identify the factors that influence customers to leave the service. Using Power BI, the project provides interactive visualizations and insights that help businesses improve customer retention strategies, reduce churn rates, and enhance customer satisfaction.

---

## Tech Stack Used

* **Power BI** – Data visualization and dashboard creation
* **Power Query** – Data cleaning and transformation
* **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Calculated measures and KPIs
* **Microsoft Excel/CSV** – Dataset handling

---

## Data Source

* Dataset obtained from **Kaggle**
* Dataset: **Telco Customer Churn Dataset**
* Contains customer demographic information, service subscriptions, contract details, payment methods, tenure, monthly charges, total charges, and churn status.

---

## Features / Highlights

### Business Problem

Telecom companies often face customer attrition (churn), which directly impacts revenue and profitability. Understanding the reasons behind customer churn is essential for developing effective retention strategies.

### Dashboard Features

* Overall customer churn rate analysis
* Customer demographics analysis (gender, senior citizens, dependents, partners)
* Internet service analysis (DSL, Fiber Optic, No Internet Service)
* Contract type and churn relationship
* Payment method analysis
* Monthly charges and total charges insights
* Customer tenure analysis
* Interactive filters and slicers for detailed exploration

---

## Key Questions Answered

1. What is the overall customer churn rate?
2. Which customer segments have the highest churn?
3. How does customer tenure impact churn?
4. Which internet service type experiences the highest churn?
5. What contract types are associated with lower churn rates?
6. How do payment methods influence customer churn?
7. Are monthly charges related to customer attrition?
8. Which demographic groups are more likely to churn?

---

## Example Insights

* Customers with **Fiber Optic** internet services show higher churn rates compared to DSL users.
* Customers on **month-to-month contracts** are more likely to leave than those on one-year or two-year contracts.
* New customers with lower tenure tend to churn more frequently.
* Electronic check payment users exhibit higher churn rates.
* Long-term contract customers demonstrate better retention.

---

## Business Impact

* Helps identify high-risk customers.
* Supports customer retention strategies.
* Enables data-driven decision-making.
* Reduces revenue loss due to customer churn.
* Improves customer satisfaction and loyalty.
